About Us

Indiana Bass'N Gals is the only all-women fishing club in Indiana. We were founded in 1985, with the mission of promoting youth, conservation, and community service. 

We currently have members from all over the State of Indiana and are looking for more women to join us! Our members encompass a variety of ages, fishing experience levels, and a mix of both boat owners and non-boat owners. We fish tournaments from April to September at varying lakes all over the State of Indiana. 

Each year we vote on what lakes to include in our tournament schedule; with the requirement of adding new lakes that we did not previously fish each year. 

We hold monthly virtual club meetings so that women from all over the state can attend. Our meetings are held on the second Monday of every month at 7pm on Google Meet. Simply reach out to us using the Contact Us page or our Facebook Page to be added to our monthly meeting. 

Youth Activities:  Our club is most well-known for our youth fishing tournament,  Kid & Co. This is our 40th annual Kid & Co. tournament. We provide a full tournament experience for kids ages 6-17: numbered takeoff and weigh-in complete with photos, cash payout, and trophies.    Hopefully this day instills a lifetime great fishing experiences for our youth.

Community Service:  The club also participates in varying community services each year.  Events include volunteering at Ronald McDonald house and donating lots of fabulous clothing to a local women's shelter. 

Conservation: We also participate in various conservation efforts each year. Each year we do a shoreline clean-up event at a local waterway.

Other club activities include: hosting a booth at the Indianapolis Boat, Sport, & Travel Show as well as handing out free fishing rods to the first 500 kids who attend the show on Kids Day.  Our time at these events allows us to network within the local fishing community, recruit new members, as well as fundraise for our club and for Kid & Co. We are proud members of the Indiana Bass Federation, The Bass Federation, and an affiliate club of the Lady Bass Anglers Association.
