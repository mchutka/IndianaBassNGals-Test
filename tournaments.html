Come fish with us!

ALL skill levels welcome and no boat is required! You will be paired with a boat owner to fish. Members-in-waiting can fish for Big Bass at only $5 per entry! Our tournament entry fee for members is affordable at just $45 with $5 going to a big bass payout.

Our club members fish together in several club tournaments throughout the season. The top eight anglers from these tournaments are chosen to compete in the Indiana Bass Federation state-wide regionals tournament. We also send two qualifying club members to the TBF national semi-finals as well as the LBAA Lady Bass Classic tournament. 

Check out our 2026 tournament schedule, past points standings, and our tournament rules and procedures below.

Come join one of our calls on the 2nd Monday of each month at 7pm EST on Zoom to learn more!
