Hello and welcome!

Visiting for the first time? Browse around to learn what the club is all about. The About Us page gives the full run-down of Indiana’s only all-women’s bass fishing club, and the Meet The Indiana Bass'N Gals page introduces the club members.

Thinking about fishing a tournament? The Tournaments page shares schedule information and club procedures. Women of all skill levels are welcome, and the Get Hooked page highlights some of the club’s catches.

The Event Calendar page shows meetings and events for the year. The Photos page gives a broader look at club activities, and the Kid & Co page highlights the club’s youth tournament. Donations and Sponsors help support that work, and the Contact page makes it easy to reach out.

Club meetings are held virtually on the second Monday of the month so members can join from anywhere in Indiana.
